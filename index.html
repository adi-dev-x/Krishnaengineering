Our Services

With over a decade of expertise in the paper and textile industries, Krishna Engineering delivers professional fabrication, erection, and commissioning services for industrial plants and machinery.

ETP Fabrication & Erection

Specialized in Effluent Treatment Plants installation as authorized contractors for SIMEM, Italy, with proven expertise across multiple installations throughout India.

Heavy Machinery Installation

Site fabrication and installation of industrial machinery including pulp mills, paper machines, and recovery plants with meticulous attention to quality standards.

Piping Systems

Comprehensive piping solutions including fabrication and erection of both M.S. and S.S. pipelines for various industrial applications, with special expertise in S.S. 316 & 317L for pulp mills.

Tank Fabrication

Specialized fabrication and erection of industrial tanks including seal tanks, blow tanks (up to 380m³ capacity), and S.S. tanks for pulp mills and recovery plants.

Industrial Maintenance

Regular maintenance contractors for paper and textile facilities, offering reliable service to keep operations running smoothly with minimal downtime.

Structural Fabrication

Design and fabrication of structural components for industrial facilities, including support structures for machinery and equipment installations.
